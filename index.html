Celestial Rapture's Release!

Celestial Rapture is an action adventure game where the player controls a hero conflicted with one task: uncover the dirty past of their parents. Set within a new solar system consumed with the same greed and corrupt thoughts that destroyed the Milky Way galaxy, the player may choose to support the government or the rebels. Fighting to piece together your parent’s past while supporting your shop proves difficult yet not unmanageable. This narrative should not suggest a clear path to choose but rather emulate a set of moral dilemmas for the player to solve. The player’s decisions should clearly affect the world.
